Refugio del Quetzal

Located in San Marcos, is a protected area with a territorial extension of 435,000 square meters, registered in the Guatemalan System of Protected Areas -SIGAP-, since 2015.


Quetzales were a symbol of light and life for the Mayans. In 1871 they became the national bird of Guatemala.

Kaybal - Parque Recreativo

Kaybal Parque Recreativo is a new natural destination that has many activities to enjoy with the family. It features llamas, interactive farms, outdoor games and much more.

Swings, tree houses, trails, campfire spaces, private parking, large gardens, horseback riding, interactive farms and a restaurant specializing in typical breakfasts.

Tajumulco Volcano

Tajumulco is the highest volcano in Guatemala, with a height of 4,222 m a.s.l. it is the highest point in Guatemala and Central America. It is the 24th most prominent volcano in the world.
